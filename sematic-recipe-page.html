Easy Banana Pancakes

Quick, fluffy pancakes made with ripe bananas — perfect for breakfast.

Serves: 2  |  Prep: 10 minutes  |  Cook: 10 minutes

  • Ingredients
  • Steps
  • Notes

Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Preparation & Cooking Steps

  1. In a bowl, mash the bananas until mostly smooth.
  2. Whisk in the egg, milk, and vanilla extract.
  3. In another bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined. A few lumps are okay.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and grease lightly with butter or oil.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake onto the pan. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
  7. Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or your favorite toppings.

Notes & Variations

For extra fluffiness, separate the egg, whip the white to soft peaks and fold in at the end. Add a pinch of cinnamon or chopped nuts for variation.

Recipe Info

Nutrition (approx.)

Per serving: ~350 kcal — varies by toppings and milk used.
